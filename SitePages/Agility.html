Overview

Last updated/Quality Checked, Feb, 2022

Agility is

  • The ability to move quickly and easily
  • The ability to think quickly and clearly
  • The ability to adapt a plan in a sustainable way based on the latest information
Business Agility is defined by the Agile Business Consortium as "agility in an organisation’s culture, leadership, strategy, and governance that adds value to all stakeholders who operate in uncertain, complex, and ambiguous environments."

Organisational Agility

Last updated/Quality Checked, Feb, 2022

To achieve organisational Agility an organisation needs achieve agility at all levels and in all activities from the teams scrum call to the portfolio sync, from the backlog refinement sessions to the strategy definition sessions. A learning culture needs to be more than just encouraged, it needs to be the oil that ensures a smoothly evolving way of working across your entire workforce
