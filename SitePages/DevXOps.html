Overview

Last updated/Quality Checked, Feb, 2022

Not another varient of DevOps that means well, DevOps? Well, in a way, your right. This is not another varient of DevOps. What it is, is a recognition that the word DevOps did not encompass everything DevOps stood for. Shifting everything left, BizOps, DevSecOps, e.t.c in the spirit of Lean-Agile where everything from ideation right through to customer value realisation, or as some like to more crudely call it 'Concept to Cach' has always been a tennant of DevOps. If you understand this then you understand that Business, Security, Compliance, Test, Operations, Development e.t.c are all part of a properly functioning DevOps ecosystem.

As with many good advancements, the name associated to it can be as much of a protaganist as any detractor. DevOps appears to be suffering a bit of this with the "its just for dev and ops" view. Does that mean BizOps is just for Business and Operations? The manifesto will say no. This is why we like the term DevXOps because X can be anything.

Note: The strike through the Dev reflects that it also represents configurations where Dev is not explicitly mentioned like BizOps

People, Processes, Principles, Practices and of course Technology

Last updated/Quality Checked, Feb, 2022

Many people focus on People, Process and Tools (or Technology) when describing how the Way of Work is or how it should be. But this is not powerful enough to describe a knowledge worker ecosystem. The fundemental flaw is the missing Way of Thinking. What are the principles that drive excelence and outcomes? Why do we practice what we practice. This is the mindset needed to achieve Kaizen in the digital world. Organisation that encourage experimentation to arrive at the right Way of Working and strive for a learning culture which promotes evolution in the Way of Thinking quickly begin to exibit capabilities synonomus with DevOps.
